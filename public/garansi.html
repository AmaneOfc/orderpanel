❓ Warranty FAQ
When can I claim warranty?
Anytime your panel is still active (not expired). Unlimited claims.
What happens after I claim?
Your panel is reset with a new password, unsuspended, and extended by 30 days from today.
Where do I find my Order ID?
Check your Dashboard → History. It starts with ORD.
My panel expired. Can I still claim?
No — warranty is only valid while the panel is active. Renew first, then claim if needed.
